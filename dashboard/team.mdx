---
title: 'Team and organization'
description: 'Invite teammates, manage members, configure your organization.'
---

Every Speko account belongs to an organization. API keys, BYOK credentials, sessions, and usage are all scoped to the organization, not to individual users.

## Invite

**Settings → Team → Invite member**. Enter their email; they receive an invitation link valid for 7 days. They sign up (or sign in if they already have an account) and are added to your organization on acceptance.

## Roles

v1 ships flat membership — every member has full access to keys, providers, sessions, and billing. Granular roles are on the roadmap.

## Two-factor

Each member can enable TOTP-based 2FA from **Profile → Security → Two-factor**. Once enabled, sign-in requires a code from their authenticator app.

## Switching organizations

If you belong to multiple organizations, the org switcher in the top-left lets you toggle between them. API keys do not move with you — each organization has its own.

## Auth options

Email + password, plus Google OAuth. Configured at the platform level.
